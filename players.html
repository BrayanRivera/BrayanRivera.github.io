 Mang0

Rank: #1
Name: Joseph Manuel Marquez
Active Years: 2005 - Present
Main Character: Fox Falco
About the Player: Joseph "Mango" Marquez is an American Fox and Falco main currently playing for Cloud9. Due to his numerous Major tournament wins, he is considered one of the greatest Melee players of all time. He is highly regarded on many regional and national Power Rankings. Mango is famous for being able to play several characters at a highly competitive level. Breaking onto the scene as a Jigglypuff main, he later found success playing Falco and Fox. A 2021 list compiled by PGStats ranked Marquez as the greatest Melee player of all time. He is one of the most popular Melee players, with an active Twitch channel that boasts over 460,000 followers.

 Hungrybox

Rank: #2
Name: Juan Manuel DeBiedma
Active Years: 2007 - Present
Main Character: Jigglypuff
About the Player: Juan "Hungrybox" Debiedma is an American and Argentinian Jigglypuff main from Orlando, Florida, currently playing for Team Liquid. His success at the highest level of competition has cemented his place as one of Melee's best players. He is widely considered one of the greatest Melee players of all time, being ranked 3rd on The Melee Stats All-Time Top 100. He has been ranked one of the top five Melee players in the world every year since formal rankings began in 2013, achieving the top rank a record three consecutive times from 2017 to 2019. An active streamer on Twitch, where he mostly streams online tournaments as either player or host, he currently has over 455,000 followers on Twitch, and over 500,000 subscribers on his YouTube channel.

 Leffen

Rank: #3
Name: William Peter Hjelte
Active Years: 2011 - Present
Main Character: Fox
About the Player: William Peter Hjelte, better known by his gamer tag Leffen, is a Swedish professional fighting game player and streamer. Although mostly known as a Super Smash Bros. Melee player, he has also competed in Dragon Ball FighterZ, Guilty Gear Strive, and Super Smash Bros. Ultimate. In Melee, Hjelte plays the character Fox, and is considered one of the best players in the world, having been ranked as one of the top six Melee players in the world every year since 2014. Since Armada's retirement in 2018, Leffen has been considered the undisputed best Melee player in Europe. A 2021 list compiled by PGStats ranked Hjelte as the sixth greatest Melee player of all time.

 Zain

Rank: #4
Name: Zain Naghmi
Active Years: 2014 - Present
Main Character: Marth
About the Player: Zain Naghmi, more commonly known by his tag Zain, is an American Super Smash Bros. Melee player from Reston, Virginia. Beginning his career in 2014, he rapidly climbed the ranks, becoming a ranked top 100 player in the world within only two years and a top 10 player since 2018. He is considered the best Marth player in the world, and one of the best two Marth players in the game's history together with Mew2King. Zain was picked up by the Golden Guardians, the official esports affiliate of the Golden State Warriors NBA team on February 6th, 2020.

 aMSa

Rank: #6
Name: Masaya Chikamoto
Active Years: 2013 - Present
Main Character: Yoshi
About the Player: aMSa, nicknamed the “hardest-working man in Melee”, is a Melee Yoshi player from Hyogo, Japan who is currently residing in Vancouver, British Columbia, Canada and is considered to be one of the best players in the world. He entered the scene in January 2013 and quickly became known as the best Yoshi player in the world after his strong performances. aMSa was formerly ranked 1st on the Japan SSBM Rank 2018. aMSa won The Big House 10 in October 2022, becoming the first player to win a Melee major with Yoshi and the second person from Asia to win a Melee major after fellow Japanese player Captain Jack's victory at MLG San Francisco 2004.
Alias:

 iBDW

Rank: #7
Name: Cody Schwab
Active Years: 2015 - Present
Main Character: Fox
About the Player: iBDW, often referred to by his first name, Cody, is a Melee smasher from Queens, New York, previously having attended college in Rochester, New York before graduating. He originally began competing out of curiosity for how far he could progress in late Spring of 2015, but after having difficulty finding practice in person he began competing on Anther's Ladder, an online website for online play, where he quickly rose to the top of the rankings. In September 2021, after almost a year of exceptionally strong performances online, iBDW would claim his first ever major tournament win at Riptide. He defeated Hungrybox 3-1, and then Plup 3-2 and 3-1 in two back to back sets to win the tournament without dropping a set.
Alias:

 Jmook

Rank: #8
Name: Jake DiRado
Active Years: 2016 - Present
Main Character: Sheik
About the Player: Jmook is a Sheik player from New York. Although he has been active in the scene since October 2014, he didn't gain prominence until 2022, where following a string of top 6 placements at majors, he cemented himself as not only the best Sheik player, but also one of the best players in the world in the post-pandemic metagame. Jmook had his breakout performance at GENESIS 8, where he took sets off, Plup, and iBDW, falling only to Zain twice for a second place finish. His debut at 3rd on the MPGR Summer 2022 is the highest placing for a previously unranked player since the inception of the Panda Global Rankings.
